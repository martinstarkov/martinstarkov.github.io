Wind Turbine

This project was for a university class. The aim was to extract power from the wind using a turbine built from scratch. My job was to design the structural elements of the turbine, such as the gear box, blade hub, casing, and more. The video shows a test of the turbine inside the university's wind tunnel.

Bayes Hopper Vehicle

The aim of this project was to create a thrust-vector-controlled lander vehicle for the student rocketry team Endeavour. My role was designing some of the structural elements of the vehicle, creating a CAD of them, as well as writing the flight computer software. The video shows the hopper gimballing around its test stand.

Fluid Simulation

This simulation was part of a work project for Codeplay. The goal was to use SYCL (parallel computing api) to accelerate the solution of the Navier-Stokes equations in 2 dimensions. I wrote the simulation and optimized it work with very fine resolutions. The video shows how granular the fluid is while maintaining a high framerate.

Game Engine

Written in C++, the Protegon 2D game engine is something I have spent multiple years working on. It is how I taught myself programming and I have iterated and improved it over time. Currently it supports drawing objects, animations, tile maps, bullet collision, physics, interfaces, scene management, and more. The video demonstrates a character moving through a procedurally generated world.
